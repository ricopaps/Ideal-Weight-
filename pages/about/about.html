About What is an Ideal Weight Calculator?

Based on height, gender, and age, the Ideal Weight Calculator calculates ideal bodyweight (IBW) ranges. For a long time, many scientists have been looking for a way to calculate the IBW using a formula. Several common formulas are still in use today, and our Ideal Weight Calculator provides their results for side-by-side comparisons..
